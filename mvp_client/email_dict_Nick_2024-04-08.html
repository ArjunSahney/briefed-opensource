2024 Solar Eclipse in Binghamton: A Memorable Spectacle
Binghamton, NY, experienced a partial solar eclipse on April 8, with peak visibility at 3:23 p.m. Local schools adjusted schedules for optimal viewing, dismissing middle and high school students at 11:30 a.m. and elementary students at 12:30 p.m. The path of totality entered southwestern New York just after 3:15 p.m. and exited northern New York before 3:30 p.m., making this event a memorable spectacle for eclipse enthusiasts across the region (AOL.com).
