### Stock Market Highlights: Tech Leads Amidst AI Advancements

Tech stocks soared as Nvidia kicked off its AI developer conference, signaling a robust day for tech companies on the stock market, led notably by Tesla, Alphabet, and Nvidia. Tesla announced a price hike for its Model Y, boosting its stock performance, while Alphabet experienced a surge upon reports of licensing discussions for its AI technology with Apple. Nvidia impressed with its new chip technology reveal, contributing to the tech-driven market rally. Additionally, Uber agreed to a substantial settlement with Australian taxi drivers, and oil prices hit a yearly high, hinting at potential future gasoline price increases (Article 1, Article 2).
