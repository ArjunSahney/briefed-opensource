### "NCAA Division II Basketball Championships 2024 Overview"

The 2024 NCAA Division II women's basketball championship is set to run from March 15 through March 29 in St. Joseph, Missouri, featuring a 64-team field with 23 automatic bids from conference champions and 41 at-large bids. Ashland is the defending champion, looking to add to their impressive history in the tournament that saw its inception in 1982. The men's championship begins a day later on March 16, concluding on March 30 in Evansville, Indiana, with a similar structure. Nova Southeastern holds the current men's title, having won their first championship in 2023. Both tournaments will be streamed live, with the early rounds available through the NCAA Championships Pass. Historic performances in past tournaments set the stage for a highly anticipated 2024 season for both men and women's teams (Article 1, Article 3).

### ACC Basketball Honors for the Davis Duo

RJ Davis has been distinguished as the ACC's top player, leading the scoring with an average of 21.1 points per game, while North Carolina's coach, Hubert Davis, bags the Coach of the Year award. Their achievements underline North Carolina's dominant season, clinching the regular-season title for the first time since the 2016-17 season. RJ Davis's performance is further highlighted by his inclusion in the All-ACC first team, showcasing the significant impact of the Davis duo on North Carolina's successful season (Article 1, Article 2, Article 3).
