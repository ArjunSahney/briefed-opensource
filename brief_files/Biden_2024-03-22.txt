### Swing State Voter Sentiments Pre-Election

CNN polling reveals voter dissatisfaction in Michigan and Pennsylvania, key states that swung blue in the 2020 election. The polls indicate a tight race between Trump and Biden, with varying support among demographic groups. Notably, a significant portion of voters are undecided, hinting at the potential for shifts before the election. Additionally, the data suggests concerns over candidate qualities and policy capabilities, with many voters expressing doubts about both Trump and Biden's suitability for presidency based on various attributes (CNN).
