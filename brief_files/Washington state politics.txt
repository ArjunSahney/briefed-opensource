### Biden's Opposition Shifts Tactics

The group protesting President Biden's policies is redirecting their efforts to Washington State, signaling a new strategy in the lead-up to the presidential primary (Article 1). This shift includes a campaign that, up to this point, has not fully committed to any single candidate or platform, highlighting an unusual approach within the Democrats' strategy in the state (Article 2). Meanwhile, the political landscape heats as House Republicans call for a public hearing with Hunter Biden, indicating increased scrutiny on the president's circle (Article 3).
